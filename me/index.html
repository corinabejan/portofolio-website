Programming

The Discovery And The Passion

I dicovered programming in august last year. It took me a long time to decide if I should start with it or not because I was afraid i won't get it. BUT I DID! I started with HTML and I was immediately absorbed by the fact that out of nothing I could now start to create something wonderful. And then... My curiosity kicked in... I immediately started to explore CSS and of course JavaScript. The beginning was easy, but step by step it started to get more complicated. It's absolutely fascinating the power of this programming language, and what how rewarding it can feel every time when a milestone is passed, and i passed a few by now. Starting coding was one of my best decissions that I ever took!

My projects over this period of time where sometimes simple, sometimes really challenging but I enjoyed every moment of this journey. The one person who never, but absolutely never let me say that anything is difficult is my best friend. She, herself, is a Software Engineer, and she is a great and inimaginably smart person. I actually believe that coding brought us much closer than we was before.Thank you Programming for making my life so much better!
